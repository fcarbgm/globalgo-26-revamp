About GlobalGo · 私たちについて

We don't just advise.
We act.

GlobalGo — 株式会社グローバルゴー — is a Fukuoka-based international business and education firm founded in 2014. We operate across five disciplines, connecting Japan with the world through deep local expertise and bilingual delivery.

We've built our reputation on one principle: results over process. Every engagement is defined by outcomes, not by hours billed or reports filed.

2014年設立。福岡県久留米市を拠点に、5つの分野で日本と世界をつなぐ国際ビジネス・教育企業です。

Mission · ミッション
Make global opportunity accessible.
Removing barriers of language, regulation, and distance for every client we serve.
言語・規制・距離の壁を取り除き、すべてのクライアントにグローバルな機会を届ける。
